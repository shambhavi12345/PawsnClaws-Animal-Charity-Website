About Us

PawsnClaws is a non-profit organization that works in collaboration with other national NGOs for helping our furry friends. Based in Solan, the organization consists of 20 enthusiastic members. Our aim is to help every animal in need and build a beautiful community as a whole. We carry out various donation and adoption drives on a small-scale in the cities of Solan and Shimla. We hire new members in the month of June.We'll be glad to have you in our team!
